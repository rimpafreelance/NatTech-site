About NatTech

EOSLe, a division of Nitesh Enterprises, is a global B2B platform committed to transforming the way businesses connect, collaborate, and grow. We seamlessly link manufacturers, suppliers, and buyers across industries and geographies through a unified, data-driven ecosystem.


Built on verified data, integrated services, and smart digital solutions, EOSLe empowers organizations to streamline their supply chains, discover new opportunities, and build lasting partnerships based on trust and transparency.


With innovation at our core, we aim to create a world where business connections are effortless, efficient, and impactful.

Our Mission & Vision

To revolutionize global B2B trade by providing a seamless digital platform that connects manufacturers, suppliers, and buyers through verified data, integrated services, and intelligent tools — enabling efficiency, transparency, and sustainable growth across industries and markets.


To become the world’s most trusted and intelligent B2B ecosystem — seamlessly connecting manufacturers, suppliers, and buyers through data-driven innovation, transparency, and collaboration that empower global business growth.
